    Natassja Neumann remembers the moment she first wanted to do hair at 5-years-old; so in 2007, she decided to grow her talent and skills by attending the Aveda Institute in Minneapolis. While she loves coloring and cutting, equally, to customize a look for her client, she believes styling is the most important part. Natassja makes sure to teach each and every one of her clients, during their appointments, how to duplicate their look at home. She considers styling and special occasion/wedding updos her biggest passion and specialty.
